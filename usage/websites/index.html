    Suggestions

    For the fans of ANSI and ASCII art, there are several websites that RetroTxt monitors in the background. These are optional.

    The only permanent domain is retrotxt.com, which is used as a fallback to confirm the Extension installation works. This domain also hosts the Sample artworks you can access by typing txt samples in the address bar.

    Sample files

    The samples are also browsable using: retrotxt.com/e

    • retrotxt.com
      Hosts the texts and artworks linked in the Samples tab.
    • localhost
      Permits the use of local and simple HTTP servers.
    • 16colo.rs
      The primary hub of the ANSI and ASCII art communities.
    • defacto2.net
      An extensive collection of computer underground text art and documents.
    • gutenberg.org
      The world's most comprehensive collection of public domain books in plain text and other formats.
    • scene.org
      The primary file hosting service for the demo scene.
    • textfiles.com
      An extensive text file collection from the early Internet and BBS era.
    • uncreativelabs.net
      Retro computing texts and files.
